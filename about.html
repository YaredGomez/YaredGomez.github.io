About

The early history of Club Deportivo Guadalajara Club Deportivo Guadalajara or
'Chivas' as They're more well know, was founded on the 8th of May 1906. The club was originally called club
'Union' but was later changed because the founder Edgar Evaraert relized that naming a club after the home
city gets more popularity. 'Chivas' is comprised of 3 colors red white and blue the colors symbolize "Fraternity,
Union and Sports" the colors were borrowed from the french flag. Chivas holds the Mexican league record for the
longest win streak with 8 consecutive wins at the start of the season. Chivas holds 12 Mexican league titles.
7 campeon de campeones or 'champion of champions' cup and 2 CONCACAF champions league cups.
