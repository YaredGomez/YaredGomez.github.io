The most loved team in Mexico

Club Deportivo Guadalajara aka 'Chivas' is one of the few clubs in the world of Futbol or soccer that only uses 'Cantera' or Mexican bred players. Chivas is the most loved club by the majority of Mexican people because of the fact that we only play with Mexicans.

  • C.D. Guadalajara was founded on the 8th of May 1906.
  • C.D. Guadalajara was ironically founded by a Belgian named Edgar Everaert.
  • C.D. Guadalajaras biggest rival is Club America who is based in Mexico city, C.D. Guadalajara also has same city rival Known as Atlas FC.
