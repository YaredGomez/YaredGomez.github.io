The most loved team in Mexico

Club deportivo Guadalajara aka 'Chivas' is one of the few clubs in the
world of Futbol or soccer that only uses 'Cantera' or Mexican bred players.
Chivas is the most loved club by the majority of Mexican people because
of the fact that we only play with Mexican people.
