The history of C.D. Guadalajara

The full name of 'Chivas' is Club deportivo Guadalajara which translated means'sporting club Guadalajara'
CD Guadalajara was founded on the 8th of May in 1906 as club name 'Club Union' Chivas plays at Akron Stadium their home stadium.
Chivas was founded by Edgar Evaraert, a Belgian which is ironic if you think about Chivas being a Mexcan only club.
Edgar Evaraert borrowed the vertical stripes and colour scheme from a belgian club called Club Brugge K.V.
Chivas first started off playing with a mix of Mexican, Belgian and French players and the reason the club
was called Club Union was becasue of the fact that there was a mix of different nationalities.
until the Mexican people got angry because foreigners were being favored more at the time
Chivas changed to being a Mexcan only club to help the opression that was happening.

Founder Edgar Evaraert
